Partying Robot! (& Cotter, an Eccentric Millionaire)

published: 12.11.2019, 12:53

I've recently finished General Assembly's free tutorials at Dash. One of my favorite projects was making a partying robot, using CSS. Your client, an eccentric millionaire named Cotter, hires you to build a robot that he can take with him when he goes partying.

You can click three different buttons. One toggles some laser eyes for the robot. The other toggles Cotter's dancing (so he can take a break). The last button changes the background color. Repeatedly pressing it creates a strobe effect! Click here to try it out!
