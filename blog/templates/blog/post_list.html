Partying Robot! (& Cotter, an Eccentric Millionaire)

published: 12.11.2019, 12:53

I've recently finished General Assembly's free tutorials at Dash. They walk you through the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (with a little jquery too). They have you build a few simple sites as you go along their tutorials.

One of my favorite projects was making a partying robot, using CSS. Your client is an eccentric millionaire named Cotter. He hires you to build a robot that he can take with him when he goes partying.

You can click three different buttons. One turns on/off some laser lights for the robot. The other turns on/off Cotter from dancing (so he can take a break). The last button changes the background color. Repeatedly pressing it creates a strobe effect. Click here to try it out!
